```
## Addendum (human review of model verdicts)

The model marked **home**, **guide**, and **runs** as "regressed". On manual
inspection these are model-side false positives:

- Indigo underline on the active nav link reads as the **same** accent
  as the primary button — not a second accent. (See §3, §6 Page header.)
- World 1 (`bg-accent-soft`) and World 2 (`bg-warn-bg`) callouts in
  /guide are deliberate categorical markers in prose, allowed under §8
  (categorical palette use beyond charts when the content is itself
  categorical).
- /runs spacing is `mt-6` between header and content as required by §5.
  The model misread the rendered gap.

These are recorded so a future re-run does not re-flag them. If a future
verdict reaches the same conclusion, re-evaluate — do not silently dismiss.
```
